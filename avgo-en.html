VI. Risk Factors

Key Risks (By Severity)

  1. Customer Concentration (Highest): AVGO's AI revenues rely heavily on a few Hyperscalers (e.g., Google, Meta). Any sudden deceleration in their CAPEX cycles will severely hit AVGO's topline.
  2. Foundry & Capacity Reliance: Silicon manufacturing is heavily dependent on TSMC's advanced nodes. Tight CoWoS packaging allocations could cap AVGO's revenue ceiling.
  3. Software Segment Stagnation: Infrastructure software grew only 1% YoY. If customer churn accelerates post-VMware integration, it could eventually drag down the overall profit structure.
  4. Geopolitics: Escalating US-China chip bans could threaten its legacy broadband and networking sales in the Chinese market.

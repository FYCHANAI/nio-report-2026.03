VI. Risk Factors

Key Risks (By Severity)

  1. Delayed AI CAPEX ROI (Highest): If data center build-outs outpace actual AI deployment by clients, or if GPU deliveries lag, margins and cash flows will suffer in the near term.
  2. Hyperscaler Price Wars: Deep-pocketed rivals like AWS and Azure could initiate aggressive pricing, compressing OCI's lucrative margins.
  3. Cerner Integration Drag: The protracted transition of healthcare operations to a SaaS model could continue to anchor down overall topline growth.
  4. Macro & FX Headwinds: Global IT budget contractions due to economic downturns, compounded by strong USD FX losses.
  5. Key-man Risk: Founder Larry Ellison's age introduces governance and strategic uncertainty during eventual succession.

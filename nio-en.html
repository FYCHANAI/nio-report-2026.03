Valuation Conclusion

Conclusion: Substantial Value Zone

NIO currently trades at a Forward P/S of ~1.1x, significantly lower than Li Auto (1.8x) and XPeng (1.5x). The market previously penalized NIO's valuation due to pessimism surrounding its heavy battery swap CAPEX.

VI. Risk Factors

Key Risks (By Severity)

  1. Intensifying Domestic Price War (Highest): Aggressive price cuts by BYD or Tesla could force ONVO to squeeze margins to defend market share.
  2. Production Hell Repetition: NIO has historically struggled with supply chain bottlenecks during new launches; delays with ONVO L60 would severely damage market confidence.
  3. Geopolitics & Tariffs: US/EU tariff walls against Chinese EVs will hinder the global expansion timeline for NIO and Firefly.
  4. Dilution from Cash Burn: Failure to rein in BaaS and R&D expenditures could trigger further equity dilution risks.

VII. Scenario Analysis

Bull Case - Target: $10.00

  • Financial Impact: Achieves company-wide Non-GAAP profitability ahead of schedule.

  • Financial Impact: Losses narrow significantly; FCF remains healthy without short-term financing needs.

Bear Case - Target: $4.00

  • Catalysts: Brutal domestic EV price war escalates; ONVO loses order backlog to aggressive competitors.
  • Financial Impact: Margins collapse below 10%, exacerbating losses and stoking fears of cash exhaustion.
  • Market Reaction: Capital flight crushes the valuation multiple to sub-0.8x P/S.

Core Logic:

The darkest hour (high R&D, low conversion, capacity bottlenecks) has passed. NIO has entered a harvest phase where massive upfront infra investments (BaaS, ADAS) are translating into mass-market scale via ONVO and Firefly.

Near-term Catalysts (1-3 Mo):
